# We're Moving from Monthly to Daily AI Limits (and Making Them More Generous)

Hey builders! 👋

We're making a change to how AI usage works in Nowa, and we think you're going to like it. In short: we're replacing **monthly credit limits with daily limits**, giving you **significantly more total usage at the same price**, and bringing **credit top-ups to the Launch plan**.

Here's the full story.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## Why we're changing things

Under the old model, Launch came with **50 credits per month** and Scale came with **200**. That sounds fine on paper, but we kept seeing the same pattern: many of you build in intense bursts. You'd pour your energy into your app, burn through your monthly credits in a few days, and then... wait. Sometimes for weeks.

That waiting is the opposite of what we want. Momentum matters when you're building. Coming back to your app every day, even for a short session, is how ideas turn into real products. So we redesigned our limits around exactly that: **showing up and building every day**.

---

## Starter (free): test Nowa every single day

The Starter plan exists so you can try Nowa risk-free and see what you can actually build before committing to anything. But with the old 5 credits per month, one enthusiastic day of experimenting could leave you locked out for weeks — not a great way to evaluate anything.

Now, Starter gives you a **free daily AI limit** instead. If you hit it, you have two options: upgrade, or simply come back tomorrow and pick up where you left off. Either way, you're never stuck waiting a month to keep exploring your idea.

---

## Launch: 12x more usage at the same price

Launch now comes with a daily limit that's **5x the Starter plan** (on top of everything else Launch includes, like deploying to Web, Android, and iOS).

Here's the part we're most excited about: if you use your Launch plan every day, that adds up to **12x more usage** than the old 50-credits-per-month plan — at the same price. Yes, 12x. You can build every single day without ever worrying about running dry for weeks.

And there's one more upgrade: **top-ups are now available on Launch**. Previously, only Scale users could purchase extra credits. Now, if you hit your daily limit on Launch and you're in the zone, you can buy a top-up and keep going.

Two things worth knowing about how top-ups work:

<div className="nowa-callout">
<div className="nowa-callout-item"><span className="nowa-callout-num">1</span><span>Nowa always uses your plan's daily credits first, so your purchased credits are only touched when you go beyond your daily limit.</span></div>
<div className="nowa-callout-item"><span className="nowa-callout-num">2</span><span>Top-up credits never expire. Buy some today, use a few, and the rest will sit safely in your account for whenever you need them.</span></div>
</div>

---

## Scale: 6x more usage at the same price

Scale gets the same treatment. Used daily, the new Scale plan gives you **6x more usage** than the old 200-credits-per-month plan — again, at the same price. Your daily limit is **2x the Launch plan**, and you keep everything that makes Scale, Scale: GitHub integration, premium support, and of course top-ups whenever you want to push past your daily limit.

---

## One credit is now simply $1

While we were at it, we simplified our credit unit. Previously, 1 credit was worth $0.50. Now, **1 credit = $1**, and each new credit does twice the work of an old one (under the hood, a credit maps to a number of AI tokens, and we doubled it).

To be clear: **nothing got more expensive.** We just redefined the unit so the math is easier for everyone.

---

## A little gift for existing users 🎁

<div className="nowa-gift">
If you already have credits in your account, here's some good news: even though the new credit is worth double the old one, we're keeping your credit numbers exactly the same. If you had 20 credits, you still have 20 credits — they just do twice as much now. <strong>Your existing balance effectively doubled, for free.</strong>
</div>

## Why this matters to us

At the end of the day, what we care about is seeing you ship impressive apps you're proud of — fast, with the power of AI, and with the full visual control and understanding of your app that Nowa gives you. This change means you can build more without paying more, and you can do it every single day instead of waiting on the sidelines.

We can't wait to see what you build.
