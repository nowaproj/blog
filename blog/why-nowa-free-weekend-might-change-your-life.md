## The real thing standing in your way isn't the tool

It's human nature. We procrastinate. We're afraid of failing. We keep waiting for the perfect moment that never comes — until the opportunity quietly slips away.

By making Nowa free for 48 hours, we want to give you some urgency to actually start moving. You have a deadline, no risk at all, and all of the upside: you could build something genuinely impressive in those two days, and once you do, stopping becomes harder than continuing.

Sometimes all you need is that first push. We're giving it to you here.

## We made it a no-brainer

To remove every excuse:

- **Every app** you build during those 48 hours gets **50 free AI credits** to keep working on it after the free weekend ends.
- The **top 3 apps** built during the weekend get **$500 worth of AI credits** to keep advancing the app — plus free exposure on our socials to get you your first users and testers.

So there's nothing to lose, and quite a lot to gain.

---

## How we'll rate the apps

When we pick the winners, we look at three things:

1. **Is your app complete?** By complete, we mean: does it solve a problem for someone? It doesn't have to be big or packed with features — it just has to solve one problem really well. A small app that solves a complete problem beats a big app that solves none of it, every time.
2. **Is your app nice to use?** Does it have a clean design and a smooth, pleasant experience?
3. **How important is the problem you're solving?** Is it something real people will genuinely care about?
